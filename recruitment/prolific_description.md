# Prolific Study Description

## Title
Pattern Learning and Insight (30 min)

## Description
In this study, you will view sequences of letters and learn to judge whether each
sequence follows a hidden pattern. You will receive feedback on your judgments and
rate your confidence as you go. The study takes approximately 30 minutes.

## Eligibility
- Fluent in English
- Desktop or laptop computer (not mobile phone)
- Ages 18+
- Normal or corrected-to-normal vision

30 minutes
